Virat Kohli

Indian Cricket Legend • Modern Master

About

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is one of the most celebrated cricketers in the world. Known for his aggressive batting style, unmatched fitness, and leadership, Kohli has redefined modern cricket. He debuted for the Indian national team in 2008 and has since achieved numerous milestones across all formats of the game.

Achievements

  • Scored over 25,000 international runs
  • Most centuries in ODIs (joint with Sachin Tendulkar)
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times
  • Captain of Indian team from 2017–2021 (Tests)
  • Led India to a historic Test series win in Australia (2018–19)
  • Recipient of Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna and Padma Shri

Legacy

Virat Kohli is widely admired for his consistency, discipline, and passion for the game. He has inspired millions through his performances and continues to be a dominant force in international cricket. His legacy as a run-machine and fierce competitor is unmatched in Indian cricket history.
